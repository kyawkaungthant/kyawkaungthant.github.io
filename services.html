Notice for library members

From 18 April 2022, in addition to using our Digital Library service, miniLibrary members can visit our Library in Yangon to browse our collection and/or borrow books and other resources from Monday to Friday (8:30am-3:30pm). non-members can also visit our Library to apply for new membership or can do so online - visit this page to learn more. All necessary Covid precautions and measures are in place to ensure the safety of our Library members and our staff. If you would like to visit us, please make an appointment by calling 09xxxxxxxxx, 09xxxxxxxxx, 09xxxxxxxxx or sending an email to miniLibrary@gmail.com. 'Book Delivery Service' is also available for our physical library members in Yangon. You can order books online and the books will be delivered to your home by a delivery service. Please check our step-by-step guide for ordering books online here. For more information about our 'Book Delivery Services', please contact us via telephone numbers or email address mentioned in the above. Please note that our library in Mandalay will remain temporarily closed to the public until further notice. However, our digital library members can continue using the services online 24/7.
